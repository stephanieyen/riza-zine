Riza

Riza is an emerging NYC-based collective of AAPI artists in their early to mid-twenties who are passionate about 2D practices, including writing, photography, and printmaking, outside of their main studies and professions.

Riza is a supportive community where young AAPI artists engage in storytelling, reflect on their cultural identities and social contexts, and build lasting relationships.
